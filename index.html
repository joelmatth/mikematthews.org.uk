Mike is a serial pioneer and entrepreneur, with a relentless streak for innovation. He loves to apply new internet-based technology to old problems in order to achieve a smarter outcome.

Mike has a consistent habit of making a difference to each of the organisations (such as IBM and Cincom Systems) that he has been part of by leading project initiatives which were just not there beforehand.

His latest passion is to drive the adoption of Web 3.0, the semantic web, whereby computers will act as intelligent agents on behalf of users to deliver seamless experiences akin to having your own mobile personal assistant.

Mike's professional experience has been gained in an international context across Europe, having lived and worked in Paris, Madrid and Johannesburg as well as the UK.
